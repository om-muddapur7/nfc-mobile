About the Temple

वक्रतुंड महाकाय सूर्यकोटि समप्रभ। निर्विघ्नं कुरुमे देव सर्वकार्येषु सर्वदा।।

History
The KCD Vidyaganapati Temple, established in the heart of Dharwad, has been a center of devotion for many decades. The temple is dedicated to Lord Ganesha, who is worshipped as the deity of intellect and remover of obstacles.

Temple Architecture
Built with traditional Dravidian architecture, the temple features intricate carvings of Lord Ganesha, symbolizing wisdom and prosperity. The temple is adorned with a beautiful central dome and artistic murals.

Location
Located in the bustling city of Dharwad, the temple attracts devotees from across the city, especially during major festivals such as Ganesh Chaturthi.
